Product 01

One API. Every Healthcare
Tender on Earth.

The HealthProcure Intel API aggregates real-time procurement data from the world's largest healthcare purchasing platforms into a single, clean REST endpoint. Query tenders worth $50B+ across 85 countries, filter by source, category, geography, value, and compliance criteria. Every data point is normalized — currencies converted to USD, categories mapped to a unified taxonomy, dates standardized to ISO 8601. Whether you're building a procurement dashboard, automating bid monitoring, or feeding data into your BI stack, our API delivers production-ready JSON in milliseconds.

API Endpoints

Endpoint Description
GET /api/v1/tenders Search and filter all tenders with pagination
GET /api/v1/tenders/:id Get full detail for a single tender
GET /api/v1/awards Search contract awards by supplier, value, date
GET /api/v1/analytics/stats Dashboard KPIs: totals, trends, top buyers
GET /api/v1/analytics/benchmarks Regional benchmarks (Pro/Enterprise)
GET /api/v1/sources List all data source portals
GET /api/v1/pricing Current plan tiers and limits

  -H "Content-Type: application/json"

Product 03

Benchmark Your Bids Against
Regional Norms

Go beyond raw data with pre-computed market intelligence. Our benchmarking engine analyzes historical procurement patterns to surface actionable metrics: average lead times from publication to deadline, typical contract values by region and category, tender-to-award conversion rates, and compliance pass rates. Available on Pro and Enterprise plans.

Average Lead Time

Days between tender publication and submission deadline, segmented by region and category. Know if 60 days is generous or tight for your market.

Average Contract Value

Historical mean contract value in USD by region and procurement category. Calibrate your pricing strategy against market norms.

Tender-to-Award Ratio

What percentage of published tenders result in a contract award? Understand competitive intensity by market.

Compliance Rate

Percentage of tenders with fully documented compliance criteria. Higher rates indicate more mature procurement markets.

Period Segmentation

All metrics available by quarter (e.g., 2024-Q3, 2025-Q1), enabling trend analysis over time. Track how lead times, values, and compliance rates evolve across reporting periods to forecast market direction.
