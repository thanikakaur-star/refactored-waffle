Connect Google Search Console

Search Console isn't connected yet. To enable it, set these environment variables on your server (Railway):

  • GSC_SERVICE_ACCOUNT_EMAIL — your service account email
  • GSC_PRIVATE_KEY — the service account private key (PEM)
  • GSC_SITE_URL — e.g. https://healthprocureintel.com/

Create a service account in Google Cloud, enable the Search Console API, then add the service account email as a user on your property in Search Console.
