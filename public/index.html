How It Works

Three Steps to Intelligence

Raw procurement data transformed into actionable insights, delivered through a clean API.

1

Scrape

Playwright crawlers systematically harvest tenders, awards, and contract data from TED, SAM.gov, WHO, and NHS portals.

2

Normalize

Raw data is cleaned, deduplicated, and enriched. Currencies convert to USD, codes map to CPV/NAICS, and regional benchmarks are computed.

3

Deliver

Access normalized data via our RESTful API or interactive dashboard. Filter by region, CPV code, value range, or deadline.
